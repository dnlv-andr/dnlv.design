Mobile App Summer 2023

Free2ask Mobile App for Drugstore Project

Free2Ask is a mobile app providing essential guidance on sex education, LGBTQ+ matters, and substance use, specifically designed for teenagers and young adults who lead active lives. As a volunteer, I collaborated closely with the Drugstore team to create an engaging and trustworthy source of vital information for their non-profit educational efforts.

The problem

The primary goal was to build a trust-based communication platform for teenagers, providing essential guidance on sex education, LGBTQ+ matters, and the dangers of substance use. The challenge was to create an engaging app that offers free consultations and crucial information on protecting oneself from diseases and the risks associated with party drugs, empowering young users to make informed decisions.

The result? An engaging, trustworthy platform that educates and empowers teenagers to navigate life’s challenges safely.
